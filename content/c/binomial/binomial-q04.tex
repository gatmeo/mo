\qitem{%
    Write down and simplify the general term in the binomial expansion of $\left(2x^2-\dfrac{d}{x^3}\right)^7$, where $d$ is a constant. 

    Given that the coefficient of $\dfrac{1}{x}$ is $-70000$, find the value of $d$.
    }{%
    ${7\choose r}2^{7-r}(-d)^rx^{14-5r}$.

    $14-5r=-1$, $r=3$. Substitute it you get $d=5$.
    }{%
    <++>
}
