\qitem{%
    Find the coefficient of $x^2$ and the constant term of $\left(4-\dfrac{1}{2x}\right)^2(2+x)^7$.
    }{%
    $(4-\frac{1}{2x})^2(2+x)^7=\left(16-\frac{4}{x}+\frac{1}{4x^2}\right)(128+448x+672x^2+560x^3+280x^4+\cdots )$

    coefficient of $x^2=16(672)+(-4)(560)+\frac{1}{4}(280)=8582$, constant term $=16(128)+(-4)(448)+\frac{1}{4}672=424$.
    }{%
