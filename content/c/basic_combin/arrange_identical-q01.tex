\qitem{%
    An ant has to go from $(0,0)$ to $(1,3)$. In each move, it can travel in the 4 directions. (i.e. it can travel from $(x,y)$ to $(x+1,y)$, $(x,y+1)$, $(x-1,y)$ or $(x,y-1)$) Find the number of ways for the ant to arrive its destination after exactly 6 steps.
    }{%
    2 Scenarios: RUUU+LR, RUUU+UD

    $\dfrac{6!}{3!2!}+\dfrac{6!}{4!}$.
    }{%
    <++>
}
