\qitem{%
    19 identical candies are distributed to 10 different students. Find the number of ways to distribute the candies, where none of the students gets more than 10 candies.

    }{%
    All - scenarios where one students gets more than 10 candies.

    All: $C^{28}_{19} $.

    One student has at least 11 candies: $C^{8+9}_{9} \cdot 10$.
    }{%
