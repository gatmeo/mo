\qitem{%
    How many ways are there to choose 3 numbers from $\left\{1,2,3,\dots,24,25\right\}$ so that the GCD of the three numbers is 1?
    }{%
    $\binom{25}{3}-\binom{12}{3}-\binom{8}{3}-\binom{5}{3}-\binom{3}{3}+\binom{4}{3}=2017$
definitions:
$\binom{25}{3}$:all the $a,b,c$
$\binom{12}{3}$:$2|a,b,c$
$\binom{8}{3}$:$3|a,b,c$
$\binom{5}{3}$:$5|a,b,c$
$\binom{3}{3}$:$7|a,b,c$
$\binom{4}{3}$:$6|a,b,c$(add them back,since they are excluded twice,both $2|a,b,c$ and $3|a,b,c$)
