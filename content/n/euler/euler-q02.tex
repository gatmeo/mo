\qitem{%
    What is the remainder when $16^{56} + 20^{56}$ is divided by 81?
    }{%
    By Eulers we have $a^{54}\equiv 1\pmod{81}$ so $16^{56}+20^{56}\equiv 16^2(16^{54})+20^2(20^{54})\equiv 16^2+20^2\equiv 656\equiv \boxed{8}\pmod{81}$
