\qitem{%
    When 73, 216, and 227 are divided by b, the remainders are the same. What is the remainder when 108 is divided by b?
    }{%
    $b | 216 - 73 = 143$ and $b | 227 - 216 = 11$, thus $b = 1, 11$.

    Thus the answer is $0, 9$.
