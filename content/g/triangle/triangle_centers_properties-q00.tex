\qitem{%
    An acute triangle $ABC$ is given with $BAC = 45^o$. Altitudes of this triangle intersect at point $H$. Prove that $| AH | = | BC |$.
    }{%
    $AH=2R\cos A$
    $BC=2R\sin A$
    Since $\sin 45^{\circ}=\cos 45^{\circ}$, $AH=BC$, as desired.
