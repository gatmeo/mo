\qitem{%
Let $x,y \geq 1$. Prove that $x\sqrt{y-1} + y\sqrt{x-1} \leq xy$.
    }{%
    $xy - x\sqrt{y-1} - y\sqrt{x-1} \geq 0$

$(x-\sqrt{x-1})(y-\sqrt{y-1}) \geq\sqrt{x-1} \cdot \sqrt{y-1}$

Let $a =\sqrt{x-1}, b =\sqrt{y-1}$

$(a^2-a+1)(b^2-b+1) \geq ab$

$(a+\frac{1}{a}-1)(b+\frac{1}{b}-1) \geq 1$, which is trivial to prove true by AM-GM
    }{%
    https://artofproblemsolving.com/community/c4t29715f4h1643715_inequality
