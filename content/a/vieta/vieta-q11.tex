\qitem{%
    Find the sum of roots of $(3x-1355)^2+4(3x-1355)+5=0$.
    }{%
    Let the roots be $x_1$ and $x_2$. And, let $y_1=3x_1-1355$ and $y_2=3x_2-1355$.

    This means, $y_1$ and $y_2$ must be the roots to $y^2+4y+5=0$,

    By Vieta's, we know that the sum of the roots of $y^2+4y+5=0$ is $-4$, so $y_1+y_2=-4$. Therefore,
    $$y_1+y_2=(3x_1-1355)+(3x_2-1355)\implies-4=3(x_1+x_2)-2710\implies x_1+x_2=\boxed{902}$$
