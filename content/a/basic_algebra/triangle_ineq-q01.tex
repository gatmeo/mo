\qitem{%
    Let $a,b$ be real numbers such that $0\leq a\leq b\leq 1$. 

    Prove that $0\leq \dfrac{b-a}{1-ab}\leq 1$.
    }{%
    Hint: Use $0\leq b\leq 1$ and $1+a>0$.

    We have 
    \begin{alignat*}{2}
        0&\leq &b(1+a)&\leq 1+a\\
        0&\leq &b-a&\leq 1-ab\\
        0&\leq &\frac{b-a}{1-ab}&\leq 1
    \end{alignat*}
    }{%
    <++>
}
