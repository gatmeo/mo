\qitem{%
    Find the remainder when $(x+1)^n$ is divided by $(x-1)^3$.
    }{%
    Note that $(x+1)^n=((x-1)+2)^n=2^n+n2^{n-1}(x-1)+n(n-1)2^{n-2}(x-1)^2+(x-1)^3(\ldots )$
    So the remainder is $2^n+n2^{n-1}(x-1)+n(n-1)2^{n-2}(x-1)^2$
